Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026

Deep Somnia ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and delete information when you use the Deep Somnia mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Deep Somnia does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the app. The app may collect the following limited data:

  • App usage preferences and settings stored locally on your device (e.g. selected sleep sounds, timer settings)
  • Push notification tokens, if you grant permission for notifications
  • Anonymous crash and diagnostic data to help us improve app stability

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide and improve the Deep Somnia app experience
  • Send you optional push notifications (only if you have opted in)
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Retention

Your app preferences and settings are stored locally on your device and are retained until you uninstall the app or clear the app data.

Push notification tokens are retained on our servers for as long as you have notifications enabled. They are automatically deleted within 30 days of you disabling notifications or uninstalling the app.

Anonymous crash and diagnostic data is retained for a maximum of 90 days, after which it is automatically deleted.

4. How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your data at any time using the following methods:

  • Uninstall the app: All locally stored preferences and settings will be permanently deleted from your device.
  • Clear app data: On your Android device, go to Settings → Apps → Deep Somnia → Storage → Clear Data.
  • Request deletion by email: Send a request to privacy@deepsomnia.app and we will delete any server-side data associated with your device within 30 days.

5. Third-Party Services

Deep Somnia uses YouTube's API Services to display sleep and relaxation content. By using the app, you also agree to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use advertising networks or analytics services that track you across other apps or websites.

6. Children's Privacy

Deep Somnia is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at privacy@deepsomnia.app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at: privacy@deepsomnia.app
